About Palmetto Services LLC of SC

Palmetto Services LLC of SC is a locally owned and operated home‑services company dedicated to helping homeowners improve, protect, and enjoy their living spaces. Based right here in South Carolina, we take pride in delivering high‑quality workmanship with a level of care and professionalism that’s hard to find today.

We specialize in a wide range of home improvement and property‑enhancement services — from interior and exterior renovations to custom carpentry, repairs, and security‑camera installations. Whether you’re updating a kitchen, refreshing a living space, or adding peace of mind with a modern surveillance system, our goal is simple: to provide reliable service and results you’ll be proud of.

What sets us apart is our commitment to honesty, clear communication, and doing the job right the first time. We treat every home as if it were our own, and every project — big or small — receives the same attention to detail. Our customers know they can count on us for quality craftsmanship, fair pricing, and a smooth, stress‑free experience from start to finish.

At Palmetto Services LLC of SC, we believe your home should be a place of comfort, security, and pride. We’re here to help you make that a reality.
